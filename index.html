Competition Info

Launch Canada is an exciting national competition that brings together university teams to design, build, and launch rockets, showcasing their engineering prowess and innovative designs. This event pushes the boundaries of student engineering, fostering creativity, teamwork, and problem-solving under real-world constraints. Teams must meet rigorous safety and design standards, just as in the aerospace industry.

For MACH, Launch Canada represents more than just a competition—it's an opportunity to test our ideas, collaborate with peers, and demonstrate cutting-edge propulsion systems. It’s a platform where engineering theory meets practical application, allowing us to refine our liquid bipropellant rocket designs and compete on a national stage.
